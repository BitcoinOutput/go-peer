HLM is a messenger based on the core of an anonymous network with theoretically provable anonymity of HLS. A feature of this messenger is the provision of anonymity of the fact of transactions (sending, receiving).

HLS is the core of an anonymous network with theoretically provable anonymity, built on the basis of friend-to-friend (F2F) connections. It is based on the "queues" model. In certain implementations, it may exhibit the property of "abstractness".

The theory of the structure of hidden systems is a scientific and theoretical article describing the development of anonymous communications and secure applications. The analysis is based on a dialectical approach that allows us to consider the patterns of development through the sum of accumulated contradictions.
